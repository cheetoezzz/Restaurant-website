Our Story

Our story began with a simple backyard barbecue shared by three cousins, fueled by laughter, good food, and cherished memories. Inspired by those moments, their father envisioned turning this passion into something greater. Drawing from their shared love for basketball, he named the venture after their team—the Santan Bulls. What started as a family tradition has now become a legacy of flavor and connection, bringing people together one barbecue at a time.
